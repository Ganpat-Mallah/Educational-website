Topics Covered

  • What is Data Science?

    Data Science is the field of study that combines domain expertise, programming skills, and knowledge of mathematics and statistics to extract meaningful insights from data.

    • Data Science lifecycle
    • Key components: Data collection, analysis, and visualization
    • Applications in business, healthcare, and more
  • Applications of Data Science

    Data Science is used across industries to make data-driven decisions:

    • Healthcare: Predicting diseases and optimizing treatment plans
    • Finance: Fraud detection and stock market prediction
    • E-commerce: Personalized recommendations and customer analysis
  • Data Science Workflow

    The Data Science workflow involves the following steps:

    • Data collection: Gathering raw data from various sources
    • Data cleaning: Preparing data for analysis
    • Data analysis: Applying statistical and computational methods
    • Visualization and communication: Presenting findings effectively
